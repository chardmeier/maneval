The data are source-side unrepeated samples from the sum of 4 contrastive test sets (ellipsis_vp, ellipsis_infl, lexical and deixis test sets). There are 2923 samples in each file. Each sample comprises of 4 consecutive sentences divided by ' _eos '.

source.txt: source-side sentences
baseline.txt: sentence-level baseline translation
docrepair.txt: DocRepair translation 
transference.txt: Transference translation
